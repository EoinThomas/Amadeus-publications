# Variational Inference for Quantum HyperNetworks

[← Back to Publications](/Amadeus-publications/)

## Abstract

This work applies variational inference techniques to quantum-inspired hypernetwork models. The paper discusses applying variational inference techniques to quantum-inspired hypernetwork models, exploring the intersection of quantum computing concepts and neural network meta-learning.

## Key Contributions

- Variational inference framework for quantum hypernetworks
- Integration of quantum computing with neural network meta-learning
- Novel approach to uncertainty quantification in quantum ML
